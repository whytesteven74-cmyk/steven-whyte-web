<Reveal>
My name is **Steven Whyte**, and I am a Mental Health Advocate, Author, and Coach dedicated to breaking the silence around men's mental health. 

My journey didn't begin on a stage or in a book. It began in the absolute silence of 4 AM, in the weight of secrets that felt too heavy to carry but too dangerous to speak. Born in 1979, my path has been a relentless navigation through trauma, the fog of addiction, and the eventual, luminous awakening that comes from facing your own shadows without flinching.
</Reveal>

---

<Reveal>
### The Early Years
For too long, I carried the weight of things I could not say. Society told me to be strong, to suppress, to "man up." This silence nearly broke me. I lived in a world where "strength" was measured by how much you could hide, rather than how much you could heal.
</Reveal>

<Reveal>
### The Turning Point
There comes a moment when the pain of staying the same becomes greater than the pain of change. My turning point came when I realized that my story—the raw, unfiltered truth of my struggles—was not a weakness. It was my greatest bridge to others. By speaking the unspoken, I found not only my own healing but a way to help others find theirs.
</Reveal>

<Reveal>
### The Mission: Unspoken Truths
The word **"Unspoken"** represents everything we carry inside but are too afraid to voice. My mission is to create a world where men feel safe to share their struggles without judgment. 

Through my writing, coaching, and the upcoming **Unspoken** platform, I aim to foster a global community of healing, connection, and authentic self-discovery. We are building a sanctuary where the silence is replaced by the soft, steady pulse of shared truth.
</Reveal>
